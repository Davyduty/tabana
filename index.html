Management

Adding a high site

A high site helps to manage your customers it can have 1 or more routers.

Overview

High sites are central management points that allow you to oversee multiple routers and customer connections efficiently. They provide centralized control and monitoring capabilities.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Plan your high site location and coverage area
  2. Install necessary hardware and equipment
  3. Configure the main management router
  4. Set up monitoring and management software
  5. Connect and configure subsidiary routers
  6. Test all connections and management features

Pro Tips

  • Choose locations with good line-of-sight coverage
  • Ensure reliable power supply and backup systems
  • Implement redundant connections where possible
  • Regular monitoring of all connected devices
